London

London, the capital city of England and the United Kingdom, is one of the world most iconic and influential cities. Known for its rich history, diverse culture, and global significance, London offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity

London is a melting pot of cultures and ethnicities, making it one of the most cosmopolitan cities in the world. This diversity is evident in its neighborhoods, cuisine, festivals, and cultural institutions.
